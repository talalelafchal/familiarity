Bind NSButton state
<p>I'm trying to bind the state of a NSButton to an objectController but i can't find in Interface Builder the Voice "State" under Binds for the button. </p> <p>Is there a way to bind this property ? </p>
<p>I assume this is a checkbox-style <code>NSButton</code>? Bind to its "value" in IB.</p>
<p>In case anyone wants to do two-way binding between <code>NSButton</code>'s state and, say, <code>NSUserDefaultsController</code> in Swift 2, here's how you can do this. All kudos to this <a href="http://stackoverflow.com/a/3223395/458356">answer</a>.</p> <pre><code>var button: NSButton! let userDefaults: NSObject = NSUserDefaultsController.sharedUserDefaultsController().values as! NSObject let options: [String:AnyObject] = [NSContinuouslyUpdatesValueBindingOption: true] button.cell!.bind("state", toObject: userDefaults, withKeyPath: "MyButtonState", options: options) userDefaults.bind("MyButtonState", toObject: button.cell!, withKeyPath: "state", options: options) </code></pre>
