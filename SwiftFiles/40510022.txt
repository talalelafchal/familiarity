After an extensive research, I finally found a solution for this problem. The second modification I did in the Runpath Search Paths is I believe actually solved this problem. Below are the things I did/modified. 1.I moved pod 'Firebase' to my test target in the pod file as shown below.:  target 'MyAppTests' do inherit! :search_paths pod 'Firebase' end 2.configureRunpath Search Paths to point to $(FRAMEWORK_SEARCH_PATHS) 3.In addition to all the above, I uninstalled my old cocoapod(1.0.2) and installed latest version (1.1.1), removed Firebase, reinstalled it.. Then pod installed and pod updated in terminal.
