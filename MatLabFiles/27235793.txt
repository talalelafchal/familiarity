How to assign values to the variables in MATLAB?
<p>I have created a variable vector of 2*2 size and i am trying to assign some values to them and use them further in my programme, but i am not sure how to do it</p> <pre><code>Rho= sym('Rh0',[2,2]); abc= [1 2; 5 6]; </code></pre> <p>what i expect in the out put is </p> <pre><code>Rho1_1=1 Rho1_2=2 Rho2_1=5 Rho2_2=6 </code></pre> <p>can some one help me to solve this ?</p>
<p>The following works; using <code>num2cell</code> to convert the array to a cell array, and then use multiple indexing to 'deal' the variables with the content of the cell array:</p> <pre><code>clear clc abc= [1 2; 5 6]; abcCell = num2cell(abc'); %// Note the transpose; needed to get correct order. [Rho1_1, Rho1_2,Rho2_1,Rho2_2] = abcCell{:} </code></pre> <p>Giving this:</p> <pre><code>Rho1_1 = 1 Rho1_2 = 2 Rho2_1 = 5 Rho2_2 = 6 </code></pre>
<pre><code>rho = sym('r',[2,2]); r1_1 = 1; r1_2 = 2; r2_1 = 5; r2_2 = 6; subs(rho) </code></pre> <p>spits out</p> <pre><code>ans = 1 2 5 6 </code></pre>
