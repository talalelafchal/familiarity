Octave/Windows: umlauts in plots displayed but not saved as image
<p>I use octave 3.8.2 (with gnuplot) under Windows. I want to write "special character" in the axis labels of plots. The umlaut 'ä' and special character µ are displayed in the figure but not saved to the image file using print. Partly, I can use the TeX command: '\mu' instead of 'µ' but for umlauts '\"a' instead of 'ä' does not work. </p> <pre><code>plot(1:10); %set (findall (gcf (), "-property", "interpreter"), "interpreter", "TeX") % does not work xlabel('Länge in µm'); ylabel('Breite in \mum'); print('umlaute.jpg', '-djpeg'); </code></pre>
<p><code>graphics_toolkit("gnuplot")</code> and <code>pngcairo</code> or <code>pdfcairo</code> produce a better output.</p> <pre><code>graphics_toolkit("gnuplot") plot(1:10) xlabel('Länge in µm') ylabel('Breite in \mum') print('umlaute.png', '-dpngcairo') # or # print('umlaute.pdf', '-dpdfcairo') </code></pre> <p>With octave 3.8.2 under linux, the ouput is <img src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/xZPqk.png" alt="pngcairo output"></p>
