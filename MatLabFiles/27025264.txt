<p>For any ND matrix in Matlab, you can simply list values from 1 to end:</p> <pre><code>&gt;&gt; a = rand(2) a = 0.3404 0.2238 0.5853 0.7513 &gt;&gt; a(1:end) ans = 0.3404 0.5853 0.2238 0.7513 </code></pre> <p>If you were looking for row-by-row, then you can transpose first:</p> <pre><code>&gt;&gt; b = a' b = 0.3404 0.5853 0.2238 0.7513 &gt;&gt; b(1:end) ans = 0.3404 0.2238 0.5853 0.7513 </code></pre>
