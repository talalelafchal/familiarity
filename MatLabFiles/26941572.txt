<p><code>meshgrid</code> is specifically for 2D or 3D data. For arbitrary n-dimensional data, the appropriately-named <a href="http://uk.mathworks.com/help/matlab/ref/ndgrid.html" rel="nofollow"><code>ndgrid</code></a> is the guy you want.</p> <p>Note that <code>meshgrid</code> is intended for working intuitively with Cartesian <em>X,Y{,Z}</em> data, so swaps the first two dimensions in the shape of its output to reflect <em>X,Y</em> order rather than <em>row,column</em>. <code>ndgrid</code>, being more general, just gives you standard multidimensional matrix order.</p>
