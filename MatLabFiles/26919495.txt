matlab: bar, how to change the edge color?
<p>I am having trouble to change the bar color, I want it to be white in the middle, and red at the edge. Looking at matlab's description</p> <p>if I do:</p> <pre><code>bar(y,'FaceColor','w','EdgeColor','r','LineWidth',1) </code></pre> <p>It should give me the above. However, when I actually run it, it only give me white graph. </p> <hr> <p>Update: my y is:</p> <pre><code>y=zeros(1,5000); y(3000)=1; y(4000)=1; </code></pre> <p>Using the above, I got....</p> <p><img src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/qlqXx.jpg" alt="enter image description here"></p>
<p>With so many bars, Matlab probably has trouble differentiating edge (<code>'EdgeColor'</code>) and fill (<code>'FaceColor'</code>) of each. After all, each complete bar is less than a screen pixel.</p> <p>I suggest you use white edge and colored fill. That works for me. It's as if <code>'FaceColor'</code> had precedence over <code>'EdgeColor'</code>.</p> <pre><code>bar(y,'FaceColor','r','EdgeColor','w','LineWidth',1) </code></pre> <p>Or better yet: replace each bar by a <em>line</em>, that is, use <a href="http://es.mathworks.com/help/matlab/ref/stem.html" rel="nofollow"><code>stem</code></a>:</p> <pre><code>stem(y,'r','marker','none') </code></pre>
