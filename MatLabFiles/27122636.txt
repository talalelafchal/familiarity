Matlab: Group legend entries for two plots
<p>I have two 5+20 arrays, which I want to display in a single plot. However, the legend should only consist of two entries, with each individual entry referring to one of displayed plots:</p> <pre><code>figure; hold on; h1 = plot(hamming_intra, 'bo'); h2 = plot(hamming_inter, 'r+'); hold off; </code></pre> <p>If I add a legend using the <code>insert legend</code> button I get a legend with 40 entries. My goal is to get a legend with two entries (one with a red cross and one with a blue dot).</p> <p><img src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/tMCcx.png" alt="enter image description here"></p> <p>The arrays <code>hamming_intra</code> and <code>hamming_inter</code>consist of double values:</p> <pre><code>&gt;&gt; whos hamming_inter Name Size Bytes Class Attributes hamming_inter 5x20 800 double </code></pre>
<p>I found a solution on mathworks forums:</p> <pre><code>figure; hold on; h1 = plot(hamming_intra, 'bo'); h2 = plot(hamming_inter, 'r+'); hold off; h = get(gca,'Children'); v = [h(1) h(21)]'; %in my case the first and the 21st entry correspond the the first entry of both plots legend(v, 'data1', 'data2'); </code></pre>
<pre><code>figure; h1 = plot(hamming_intra, 'bo'); hold on h2 = plot(hamming_inter, 'r+'); legend([h1, h2],'hamming_intra','hamming_inter') </code></pre>
