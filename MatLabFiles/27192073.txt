Find a vector components value in matlab
<p>I have thw following problem</p> <p>Considering the matrix</p> <pre><code>M = [1 2 3; 4 5 6; 7 8 10] </code></pre> <p>And the vector</p> <pre><code>E = [Ex; Ey; Ez] </code></pre> <p>And</p> <pre><code>B = [1; 4; 10] </code></pre> <p>Does anyone knows how to find the values of E components in matlab, considering </p> <pre><code>M*E=B? </code></pre>
<p>Use the following command, using <a href="http://nl.mathworks.com/help/matlab/ref/mldivide.html?refresh=true" rel="nofollow">mldivide</a> or the equivalent operator <code>\</code>:</p> <pre><code>E = M\B </code></pre> <p>This results in:</p> <pre><code>E = 4.0000 -6.0000 3.0000 </code></pre> <p>Verification:</p> <pre><code>M*E </code></pre> <p>ans =</p> <pre><code> 1.0000 4.0000 10.0000 </code></pre>
