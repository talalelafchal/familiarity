<p>To subtract the first row from an entire array, use <code>bsxfun</code>:</p> <pre><code>A = [ 12200 9400 12200 9400 12200 9400 12200 9400 12200 9400 12200 9400 12300 9400 12300 9400 12300 9400 12300 9300 12300 9300] differenceToFirstPoint = bsxfun(@minus, A, A(1,:)); %# to calculate the norm: normOfDifference = sqrt( sum( differenceToFirstPoint.^2, 2)); </code></pre>
<p>I assume that your question is, starting from a set of points <code>(x1,y1);(x2,y2);...(xn,yn)</code> how to get <code>(0,0);(x2-x1,y2-y1);...(xn-x1,yn-y1)</code></p> <p>Quick solution: if <code>a</code> is a N-by-2 array containing your data, then</p> <pre><code>b=a; b(:,1)=b(:,1)-b(1,1); b(:,2)=b(:,2)-b(1,2); </code></pre> <p>If your question was about how to <em>import</em> the data from a CSV file or something else, it is completely different.</p>
