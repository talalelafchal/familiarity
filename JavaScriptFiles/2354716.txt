<p>If you don't want to make an explicit JavaScript function, here you can do it in just one line:</p> <p>Convert to lower and upper case respectively:</p> <pre><code>&lt;asp:TextBox ID="txt1" onblur='this.value = this.value.toLowerCase();'&gt;&lt;/asp:TextBox&gt; &lt;asp:TextBox ID="txt1" onblur='this.value = this.value.toUpperCase();'&gt;&lt;/asp:TextBox&gt; </code></pre>
