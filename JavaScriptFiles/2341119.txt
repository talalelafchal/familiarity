<p>Simplest way, concatenate it with an empty string:</p> <pre><code>325.234 + ''; </code></pre> <p>Which will implicitly call <code>toString()</code> on the number.</p> <p>You could also use:</p> <pre><code>String(325.234); </code></pre> <p>And:</p> <pre><code>325.234.toString(); </code></pre>
