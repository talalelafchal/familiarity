<p>Use another attribute on the option:</p> <pre><code>&lt;option value="Black/White" rel="images/taylormade_purelite_standbag_bk.jpg"&gt;Black/White&lt;/option&gt; </code></pre> <p>Then get that attribute with <code>getAttribute("rel")</code></p> <pre><code>&lt;select name="Color:" onchange="document.getElementById('myimage').src = this.options[this.selectedIndex].getAttribute('rel');"&gt; </code></pre>
<p>Setting up a mapping from your color options to the URLs might work, I think.</p> <pre><code>&lt;script&gt; var colorUrlMap = { "Black/White" : "images/taylormade_purelite_standbag_bk.jpg", //Add more here "White/Red/Black" : "images/taylormade_purelite_standbag_wrb.jpg" }; &lt;/script&gt; &lt;select name="Color:"onchange="document.getElementById('myimage').src = colorUrlMap[this.value];"&gt; &lt;option value="Black/White"&gt;Black/White&lt;/option&gt; &lt;!-- Add more here --&gt; &lt;option value="White/Red/Black"&gt;White/Red/Black&lt;/option&gt; &lt;/select&gt; &lt;img src="images/taylormade_purelite_standbag_bk.jpg" id="myimage"&gt; </code></pre>
