how to change the array [1,2,3,4] to [1,3,4]
<p>any simply way ?</p> <p>this is my code:</p> <pre><code>var a=[1,2,3,4] a.slice(0,1) alert( a) </code></pre> <p>and it print [1,2,3,4]</p> <p>thanks</p>
<p>You want the <a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en/Core_JavaScript_1.5_Reference/Global_Objects/Array/splice" rel="nofollow noreferrer">splice</a> method.</p>
<p>You're looking for the <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/wctc5k7s(v=VS.85).aspx" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>splice()</code></a> method:</p> <pre><code>var a=[1,2,3,4]; a.splice(1,1); alert(a); // -&gt; 1,3,4 </code></pre>
<p>You should rather use <code>splice</code> instead of <code>slice</code>:</p> <pre><code>var a = [1,2,3,4]; a.splice(1, 1); alert(a); </code></pre>
