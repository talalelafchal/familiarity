Why is this javascript getting permission denied?
<blockquote> <pre><code>Permission denied for &lt;http://example.com&gt; to get property </code></pre> <p>HTMLDocument.body from <a href="http://www.example.com" rel="nofollow noreferrer">http://www.example.com</a>.</p> </blockquote> <pre><code>var c = parseFloat(cf.contentDocument.body.offsetHeight) + 30; </code></pre>
<p>I believe this is due to the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Same_origin_policy" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Same Origin Policy</a> implemented by most (all??) browsers. Simply put <code>example.com</code> is not the same origin as <code>www.example.com</code>.</p>
<p>With a little guessing about your question, I see 2 possibilies:</p> <ol> <li><p>Javascript will not let you read data from other domains via Ajax, and you read from a domain different than example.com</p></li> <li><p>You read data from example.com, but you really try to read from www.example.com, and the webserver only serves on www.example.com.</p></li> </ol>
