<p>If you mean you do not want anything but an integer or a decimal to be typed into the field, you'll need to look at the value as each key is pressed. To catch pasted input, check it again onchange.</p> <pre><code>textbox.onkeyup=textbox.onchange=function(e){ e= window.event? event.srcElement: e.target; var v= e.value; while(v &amp;&amp; parseFloat(v)!= v) v= v.slice(0, -1); e.value= v; } </code></pre>
