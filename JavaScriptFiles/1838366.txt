JS spam protection
<p>How to use JS spam protection on email to hide the form from spam bots.</p>
<p>You don't. Putting JavaScript into email is a very bad idea for potential security and privacy violations. Instead include a link in your emails to the form on a web page. Email is inherently private and the web is inherently public. If exposing the form to a user is not a private event then it should not go in an email. </p>
