<p>Use the @{} notation to "dereference" the hash reference to the array. Note that using "use strict" at the top of your perl file is good practice and will go a long way to improving perl coding style :)</p> <p>Try something like this:</p> <pre><code>use strict; my %grade_two; $grade_two{Student}{Subject}{Test}{'Score and edited Score'} = (); push(@{$grade_two{Student}{Subject}{Test}{'Score and edited Score'}}, "123"); push(@{$grade_two{Student}{Subject}{Test}{'Score and edited Score'}}, "456"); 1; </code></pre>
