Depending on what you really want to do, your options are:</p> <ol> <li>Use <a href="http://search.cpan.org/~dougw/Spreadsheet-ParseExcel-0.65/" rel="nofollow">Spreadsheet::ParseExcel</a></li> <li>Use <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/windows/desktop/ms676539%28v=vs.85%29.aspx" rel="nofollow">ADO</a> (perhaps via DBI)</li> <li>(Install and) use <a href="https://www.openoffice.org/" rel="nofollow">OpenOffice</a>/<a href="http://www.libreoffice.org/" rel="nofollow">LibreOffice</a> if you need more than data access.
