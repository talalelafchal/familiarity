<p>Per the <a href="https://metacpan.org/pod/distribution/Time-Out/Out.pod">Time::Out</a> documentation, it does not work for blocking IO on windows. This means - at a minimum - that the tests will fail. You can skip the tests from the cpan shell using <code>force install Time::Out</code>, but this may mean that it doesn't work for your purposes.</p>
