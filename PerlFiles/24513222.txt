Once column is adding on a link to a previous column (easy enough) </p> <p>Which will be a formula(string) in the first column is <code>=HYPERLINK(B2,C2)</code> and I want to increment the 2's to 3's,4's and so on.</p> <p>Example:</p> <hr> <pre><code>=HYPERLINK(B2,C2) </code></pre> <hr> <pre><code>=HYPERLINK(B3,C3) </code></pre> <hr> <pre><code>=HYPERLINK(B4,C4) </code></pre> <hr> <pre><code>=HYPERLINK(B5,C5) </code></pre> <hr> <pre><code>=HYPERLINK(B6,C6) </code></pre> <p>It is my second day coding with sed and awk.</p> <p>Is there any way I can make this happen using awk and sed? </p>
<p>This Perl one-liner:</p> <pre><code>perl -pe "BEGIN{$i = 2} s@^@=HYPERLINK(B${i},C${i})@; $i++" "input.txt" </code></pre> <p>will add <code>=HYPERLINK(B2,C2)</code> to the front of each line and increment the numbers each time.</p>
