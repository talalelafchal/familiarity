UIBlurEffect) Please do not suggest any third party library :)
You are setting the background view to blurredBackgroundView, and then to noDataLabel. By setting it to noDataLabel, you are getting rid of blurredBackgroundView. I'd try adding noDataLabel as a subview of the background view. Like: tableView.backgroundView.addSubview(noDataLabel)
