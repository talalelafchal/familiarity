If the <Maximum depth exceeded> in the XCode console output is what you fear, fear no more. It's just a way the console is telling you that the object hierarchy is too deep for showing it to you, but the object(s) are there accessible through code. Just try to print the content of your result by iterating through it and printing each child object instead of printing only the root.
